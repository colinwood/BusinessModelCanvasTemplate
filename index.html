Revenue Streams

  • Car and Classified websites will be able to have custom advertisements of listings in the neighborhood of the group, making sales much more convienent and local

  • Insurance companies would advertise on the site, for renter's, property insurance, and auto insurance. They could offer competitive discounts to the users, since the site is technically providing better security for their property.
